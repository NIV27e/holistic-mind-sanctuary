About Holistic Mind Sanctuary

Holistic Mind Sanctuary is a pioneering wellness center located in the heart of Glasgow Green. Our mission is to integrate holistic health services within an urban park setting, focusing on mental, physical, and spiritual well-being. We are committed to combining traditional and modern healing practices to provide comprehensive wellness care to our community.

Founded by a team of experienced health practitioners and community advocates, Holistic Mind Sanctuary aims to foster a balanced lifestyle and promote sustainable health practices. Our facilities are designed with the principles of environmental sustainability, featuring green roofs, natural building materials, and spaces that harmonize with nature.

Our Values

  • Community: Building strong, supportive relationships within our community.
  • Sustainability: Promoting practices that preserve and enhance environmental health.
  • Well-being: Focusing on holistic approaches to health and wellness.
