Project Overview

The Holistic Mind Sanctuary integrates holistic health services within an urban park setting, focusing on mental, physical, and spiritual health. Our approach blends traditional and modern healing practices to provide comprehensive wellness care.

Design Philosophy

Our design is sustainable, reflecting Gaelic symbols and utilizing natural materials like wood and stone. The sanctuary emphasizes green roofs, rainwater harvesting, and renewable energy to minimize environmental impact while promoting well-being.
